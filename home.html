DOOM Eternal

By id software/ Bethesta

Doom 5 or formerly know as Doom Eternal is a sequel to the 2016 Doom reboot. The Doom slayer moves faster and is more agile than in previous Doom games. The mobility has been increased by adding the ability to dash forward, backward and to the sides. Besides that the famous Super Shotgun has gotten a grappling hook witch can be used for swinging yourself around or dragging in your enemies.

Doom Eternal is set 8 months after Doom 2016, where the demonic invasion has conquered about 60% of Earth. With the military helpless against the demonic invasion and the majority of earth's population getting killed during the first month, the ARC and the Allied Nations are created to preserve humanity and evacuate them into orbit. And during this the Doom Slayer returns from Hell with a mission to kill all the Hell Priests and save humanity.
